A dental extraction is the removal of one or more teeth from the dental alveolar bone.

Dentures/Partials

Dentures/Partials

×

A denture is a removable replacement for missing teeth.  Two types of dentures are available -- complete and partial.

Complete dentures are used when all of the teeth are missing, while partial dentures are used when some natural teeth remain.

Crowns/Bridges

Crowns/Bridges

×

A crown is a cover that is put over a tooth.  The crown restores the tooth to its normal shape, size and function.  A crown can make the tooth stronger and improve its appearance.

Dental bridges restore the gap created by one or more missing teeth.  A bridge is made up of two or more crowns and one or more false teeth known as pontics.

Ozone Trays

Ozone Trays

×

Ozone trays are made to treat different dental conditions.  Ozone therapy effectively and safely disinfects the treated area as well as promotes better blood flow and enhances immune and healing response.

Mercury Filling Removal
